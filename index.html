about the company

who are we?

our story

We began with only seven workers in 2001 and now we have over hundred workers. For the past years, we are one of the major and well established electric switch gear companies in Pakistan. We are ISO 9001:2008 certified and our panels are approved by standards of ASTA, DEWA, SEWA & ADDC.

our infrastructure

The company's infrastructure has enabled us to organize material resources, equipment and labour to execute major projects and capable of doing the maintenance for the same. Infrastructure also includes a team of experienced sales and marketing personnel backed up with well qualified design and production group.

our location

Our main office is located in Rawalpindi and our branches are located in Karachi, Lahore & Multan. Our factories are located in Ralwapindi and Lahore.
